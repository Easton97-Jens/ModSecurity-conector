# Audit architecture and evidence

**Language:** English | [Deutsch](architecture-and-evidence.de.md)

This audit index records the boundary between static checks, CI results, and
runtime evidence. A green CI workflow proves only the named check on its exact
revision; it does not promote a connector, protocol, or CRS/MRTS result.

Use [Change Records](change-records/README.md) for material change decisions
and [current reports](../current/readiness.md) for their explicitly bounded
status claims.
